Why MD-Chat exists

France has Tchap (600k+ civil servants mandated). Germany has BwMessenger and gematik TI-Messenger (74M healthcare users). Belgium has BEAM. Italy has IO. Ukraine has Diia.

Moldova — an EU candidate since 2022, with ~2.5M citizens + 1M+ diaspora — has none of this. The communication infrastructure is dominated by Telegram (politically fragile after the August 2024 Durov arrest), Viber (Rakuten-owned, declining product investment), and WhatsApp (Meta).

In 2026, this is a national security risk.

MD-Chat is the answer. Open source. EU-compliant from Day 1. Built in Moldova IT Park. Bootstrapped on $0 cash + sweat equity + EU grant ladder.

What makes MD-Chat different

1.

Verified Authentic Digital Twin

An AI clone of you, attested via eIDAS qualified signature. Responds 24/7 in your style. Revocable. Audit trail. No Western messenger has this.

2.

Confidential AI on group chats

Group summarization, sentiment, action items — running in a verifiable enclave. We never see your content. Apple PCC pattern, applied to messaging.

3.

MCP-first bot ecosystem

Every bot is also a Model Context Protocol server. Callable by Claude, GPT, any LLM externally. Inverse of WeChat's walled garden.
