# MD-Chat launches Moldova's first sovereign messenger, built to EU standards

**CHISINAU, Moldova — May 17, 2026** — Mega Promoting SRL today announced the launch of MD-Chat, the first sovereign messenger designed in Moldova and built to European Union standards for end-to-end encryption, the AI Act, and eIDAS. The application gives citizens, businesses, and public institutions in the Republic of Moldova a real alternative to foreign messaging platforms that currently dominate everyday communication, with no local control over data and no clear legal guarantees.

MD-Chat combines an end-to-end encrypted (E2EE) messaging core with a natively bilingual experience in Romanian and Russian, integration with Moldova's government identity portal EVO/MPass for verified sign-in, and an optional artificial intelligence layer hosted on local infrastructure. All core components are open source, with the codebase published on GitHub for independent audit.

"Moldova needs a digital infrastructure it controls. Messaging is not a detail — it is the backbone of how citizens, companies, and the state communicate. We built MD-Chat so that this backbone belongs to us, not to a shareholder on another continent," said Oleg Chetrean, founder of Mega Promoting SRL.

The initial launch includes clients for iOS, Android, and the web, with encrypted voice and video calls, secure document sharing, programmable bots for businesses, and embedded AI assistants for public services. All user data from Moldova is hosted exclusively within Moldova and the European Union, in compliance with Moldovan Law 195/2024 on personal data protection and with the EU's General Data Protection Regulation.

MD-Chat is free for individual users. MD-Chat Business and MD-Chat Enterprise tiers offer additional features for companies and public administration, including on-premise deployments aligned with SecNumCloud and ISO 27001 requirements.

Mega Promoting is already working with Moldova's e-Government Agency to integrate MD-Chat with digital public services, with verified official channels for ministries, municipalities, and educational institutions scheduled for pilot in the next six months.

## About Mega Promoting SRL

Mega Promoting SRL is a Moldovan technology company founded by Oleg Chetrean, specialized in conversational AI, voice agents, and sovereign infrastructure for public services.
